Born as Taylor Alison Swift on December 13, 1989, Swift is an American singer-songwriter, director, and actress. She started singing at a very young age and started her career as a country pop star at 16. Her first three albums were mainly influenced by country, and by her fourth album, "Red", her music started having pop-influenced sounds. She rose to fame after the release of her sophomore album, "Fearless". After the released of her albums, "Speak Now", "Red", "1989", she cemented herself as one of the rising stars in the industry.
She is also one of the female artists with the most grammys, with her having 12. In addition to her music career, she has also acted in movies such as "Cats", "The Lorax", "Amsterdam", and many other films. She also made her directorial debut, after she came out with a short film for her song, "All Too Well (Ten Minute Version) (Taylor's Version) (From The Vault)". Overall, she is known for her catchy songs, relatable lyrics, and being one of the most popular and influential artists during her time.

The More Fearless (Taylor's Version) Chapter (2023)

The More Fearless (Taylor's Version) Chapter is Swift's third EP. It was released on March 17, 2023. This EP, together with "The More Lover Chapter" and "The More Red (Taylor's Version) Chapter", were released in celebration of the start of "The Eras Tour". The EP includes "If This Was A Movie (Taylor's Version)", a track originally from Speak Now, and 4 other songs from her re-recorded album, Fearless (Taylor's Version). It has 5 tracks, namely:
  1. Fearless (Taylor's Version)
  2. Love Story (Taylor's Version)
  3. White Horse (Taylor's Version)
  4. You Belong With Me (Taylor's Version)
  5. If This Was A Movie (Taylor's Version)

The More Lover Chapter (2023)

The More Lover Chapter is Swift's fourth EP. It was released on March 17, 2023. The More Red (Taylor's Version) Chapter (2023)
