Phoebe Bridgers, born as Phoebe Lucille Bridgers on August 17, 1994, is an American singer-songwriter. She learned to play a guitar at 13 and started performing with it to earn money. She worked as a solo act in many locations in California, mainly in Los Angeles. Her rise to fame started when she released her debut album, "Stranger in the Alps".
In 2020, she released her second album, "Punisher", which earned her four Grammy nominations. She is also in a group called "boygenius", which consists of Bridgers herself, Lucy Dacus, and Julien Baker. Aside from boygenius, she also a part of the band "Better Oblivion Community Center". With just her two albums and six EPs, she has established herself as one of the most talented artists in the industry.

Her work

Albums

Stranger in the Alps (2017)

Stranger in the Alps is Bridger's debut album. It was released on September 22, 2017. The tracks of the album discuss topics such as mental health, anxiety, and loss. The album cover is a childhood photo of Bridgers with a drawing of a ghost covering her. This ghost represents the ghosts she faces in her lyrics. The album's genre is described to be a mix of indie rock and indie folk. It has 13 tracks, namely:
  1. Smoke Signals
  2. Motion Sickness
  3. Funeral
  4. Demi Moore
  5. Scott Street
  6. Killer
  7. Georgia
  8. Chelsea
  9. Would You Rather
  10. You Missed My Heart
  11. Smoke Signals (Reprise)
  12. It'll All Work Out - bonus track
  13. Motion Sickness (demo) - bonus track

Punisher (2020)

Punisher is Bridger's sophomore album. It was released on June 18, 2020. This album earned her 4 Grammy nominations, namely for "Best New Artist", "Best Rock Performance", "Best Rock Song", and "Best Alternative Music Album". The album's genre is described to be a mix of indie rock, indie folk, indie pop, and emo-folk. It has 11 tracks, namely:
  1. DVD Menu
  2. Garden Song
  3. Kyoto
  4. Punisher
  5. Halloween
  6. Chinese Satellite
  7. Moon Song
  8. Savior Complex
  9. ICU
  10. Graceland Too
  11. I Know The End

EPs

Killer (2014)

Killer is Bridger's first EP. It was released on March 31, 2014. Three of the tracks were eventually released on Bridger's debut album, Stranger in the Alps. It has 5 tracks, namely:
  1. Chelsea
  2. Georgia
  3. Killer
  4. Turned Around
  5. Whatever

Killer (2015)

Killer is Bridger's second EP. It was released on April 28, 2015. Two of the tracks were re-recorded songs from her first EP. Aside form the two songs, she released a new song titled "Steamroller". It has 3 tracks, namely:
  1. Killer
  2. Georgia
  3. Steamroller

Boygenius (2018)

Boygenuis is Bridger's third EP. It was released on October 26, 2018. She released this EP together with Julien Baker and Lucy Dacus. The album's genre is described to be indie rock, rock, and folk rock. It has 6 tracks, namely:
  1. Bite The Hand
  2. Me & My Dog
  3. Souvenir
  4. Stay Down
  5. Salt In The Wound
  6. Ketchum, ID

Copycat Killer (2020)

Copycat Killer is Bridger's fourth EP. It was released on November 20, 2020. The EP includes remixes of her songs from her album, "Punisher". The songs are remixed by instrumentalist and arranger Rob Moose. It has 4 tracks, namely:
  1. Kyoto (Copycat Killer Version)
  2. Savior Complex (Copycat Killer Version)
  3. Chinese Satellite (Copycat Killer Version)
  4. Punisher (Copycat Killer Version)

If We Make It Through December (2020)

If We Make It Through December is Bridger's fifth EP. It was released on November 24, 2020. The EP includes a cover of the 1973 Merle Haggard song, "If We Make It Through December", and three other songs made by Bridgers herself. It has 4 tracks, namely:
  1. If We Make It Through December
  2. 7 O'Clock News / Silent Night
  3. Christmas Song
  4. Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas

Day After Tomorrow (2021)

Day After Tomorrow is Bridger's sixth EP. It was released November 30, 2021. The EP includes "Day After Tomorrow", a newly released song, and 4 other songs, all from her previous EP, "If We Make It Through December". It has 5 tracks, namely:
  1. Day After Tomorrow
  2. If We Make It Through December
  3. 7 O'Clock News / Silent Night
  4. Christmas Song
  5. Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas

So Much Wine (2022)

So Much Wine is Bridger's seventh EP. It was released on november 17, 2022. The EP includes "So Much Wine", a newly released song, and 5 others songs, all from her previous EP, "Day After Tomorrow". It has 6 tracks, namely:
  1. So Much Wine
  2. Day After Tomorrow
  3. If We Make It Through December
  4. 7 O'Clock News / Silent Night
  5. Christmas Song
  6. Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas
